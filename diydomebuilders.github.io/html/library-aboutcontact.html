About/Contact

My encounter with geodesic domes began with the hopes of building an offgrid home. Not sure how to build a roof, I discovered geodesic domes could make that possible.
After embarking on the project, I had a lot of questions that needed some research.
Once finished, I wanted to share that research as an easy-to-use and ad-free website.
I had also found a lot of other interesting projects while researching geodesic domes.
This inspired the name of the site and have a devoted catalog for domebuilders called diydomebuilders+
